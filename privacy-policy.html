My Private Notes for Jira ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Atlassian Marketplace app.

TL;DR: Your notes stay completely private. We never access, read, sell, or share your data. Everything is stored securely within Atlassian's infrastructure.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Our app allows you to create private notes associated with Jira issues. Here's what you need to know:

  • Note Content: The text you write in your private notes.
  • Account Identifier: Your Atlassian Account ID (used only to associate notes with your account).
  • Issue Context: The Jira issue key associated with each note.

All data is stored using Atlassian's Forge Storage API within their secure infrastructure. We do not operate any external servers.

4. Data Retention

Your notes are retained as long as the app is installed on your Jira instance.

  • If you uninstall the app, your data remains in Atlassian's storage until their standard data retention policies clear it.
  • You can delete individual notes at any time using the "Clear" button in the app.
  • If you reinstall the app, your previously saved notes will still be available.
