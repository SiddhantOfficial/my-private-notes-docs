Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

My Private Notes for Jira ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Atlassian Marketplace app.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Our app allows you to create private notes associated with Jira issues. Here's what you need to know about your data:

‚úÖ Privacy by Design: All notes are stored securely using Atlassian's hosted storage (Forge Storage API). We do NOT transmit or store your data on any external third-party servers.
Data Type Description Storage Location
Note Content The text content of your private notes Atlassian Forge Storage
Atlassian Account ID Used to ensure only you can access your notes Atlassian Forge Storage
Issue Key Associates notes with specific Jira issues Atlassian Forge Storage

2. Data Usage

We use your data solely to provide the functionality of the app‚Äîspecifically, to allow you to save and retrieve your private notes.

We Do NOT:
  • Analyze your note contents
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with anyone
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Transfer data outside the Atlassian ecosystem

3. Data Access and Privacy

Your notes are keyed to your unique Atlassian Account ID. This means:

  • Only YOU can see your notes ‚Äî no one else.
  • Jira Administrators cannot access your notes ‚Äî the storage is user-specific.
  • Other users on your Jira instance cannot see your notes ‚Äî each user has completely separate storage.
  • Even we (the app developers) cannot access your notes ‚Äî data is encrypted within Atlassian's infrastructure.

4. Data Retention

Your notes are retained as long as the app is installed on your Jira instance.

  • If you uninstall the app, your data remains in Atlassian's storage until their standard data retention policies clear it.
  • You can delete individual notes at any time using the "Clear" button in the app.
  • If you reinstall the app, your previously saved notes will still be available.

5. GDPR Compliance

We are committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). As a data processor:

  • All data processing occurs within Atlassian's EU-compliant infrastructure.
  • You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data at any time.
  • You can export or clear your notes directly through the app interface.

6. CCPA Compliance

For California residents, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • You have the right to know what data we collect (as described above).
  • You have the right to delete your data at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. All functionality is contained within the Atlassian ecosystem.

8. Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • All data is stored using Atlassian's Forge Storage API, which provides enterprise-grade security.
  • Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard TLS.
  • No external servers are involved in data storage or processing.

For more details, see our Security Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
