‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can my teammates see my notes?
No. Notes are cryptographically bound to your Atlassian account. They are invisible to all other users on your Jira instance.
Can my Jira administrator read my notes?
No. The app uses user-specific storage keys that prevent access by any other account, including Jira administrators.
Is there a character limit?
Yes, approximately 32KB per note (roughly 5,000-8,000 words per issue), which is more than enough for detailed notes.
Does the app work on Jira Mobile?
The app is optimized for the Jira Cloud web interface. Mobile support depends on Atlassian's Forge rendering capabilities.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored securely within the Atlassian ecosystem using Forge Storage API. We do not transmit or store your data on any external servers.
