FAQ

Can my teammates see my notes?

No. Notes are cryptographically bound to your Atlassian account. They are invisible to all other users, including administrators.

Can Jira administrators read my notes?

No. The app uses user-specific storage keys that prevent access by any other account, including Jira administrators.

Is there a character limit?

Yes, approximately 32KB per note (roughly 5,000-8,000 words per issue).

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored securely within Atlassian's infrastructure using Forge Storage API. No external servers.
