Before Contacting Support

Please include the following in your request:

  • Your Jira Cloud instance URL
  • Browser you're using
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Any error messages
  • Screenshots if applicable

Quick Troubleshooting

Notes Not Saving?

Try refreshing the page. Check your internet connection and clear browser cache.

Panel Not Appearing?

Verify the app is installed. Admins can check in "Manage apps".

Error Messages?

Take a screenshot and include it in your support request.
