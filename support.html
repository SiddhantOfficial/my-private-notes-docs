Before Contacting Support

To help us assist you faster, please include the following information in your support request:

  • Your Jira Cloud instance URL
  • The browser you're using (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • A description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable)
  • Any error messages you've seen
  • Screenshots (if helpful)

üîß Quick Troubleshooting

Notes Not Saving?

Try refreshing the Jira issue page. If the problem persists, check your internet connection and try clearing your browser cache.

Panel Not Appearing?

Make sure the app is properly installed on your Jira instance. Jira administrators can verify this in the "Manage apps" section.

Error Messages?

Take a screenshot of the error and include it in your support request. This helps us diagnose the issue quickly.

Feature Requests

We love hearing from our users! If you have ideas for new features or improvements, please email us at support@technicaldost.com with the subject line "Feature Request".

Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to security@technicaldost.com. We take security seriously and will respond within 24 hours.
